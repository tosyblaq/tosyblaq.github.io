Our Pricing

How much will it cost me to do business with sunny oke?

Price is one of the main factors businesses consider before making a major decision.

We understand that you want to know about our pricing before deciding if we are the right fit for your budget.

Providing one-size-fits-all prices can be challenging because there are so many factors that could affect the final cost.

So, here is an overview of how we charge our clients based on the services they request from us.

WEBSITE DESIGN / DEVELOPMENT SERVICES

SUNNY OKE designs and develops complete custom-built websites for all different types of organizations using the CMS platform like WordPress. This will include all webpages plus blog and resources page.

A timeframe of 3-6 months, broken down accordingly, will typically be expected for foundational website development and UI/UX designs:

Phase 1: Web Strategy 
 
We start by understanding your business – your brand, your history, your unique selling points, and of course, your goals so that we can help you achieve those goals in ways that align with who you are as an organization and also the content strategy. This is the first month and it cost $2,000.

Phase 2: UI/UX DESIGN 
 
After gathering all of the information from Phase 1, we work to design and conceptualize how this website will function. This process can take up to six weeks, costing around $5,000.

Phase 3: Development Phase 
 
Once you approve the proposed design of the website, we will start to focus on developing it. Quality control and ensuring your visitors have a great time are all important parts of this stage; it usually lasts 8-10 weeks and costs $8,000.

Total Cost: $15,000.

So, the total cost of building a website with Sunny Oke is at least $15,000. Please note that these pricing structures are general guidelines, and the actual pricing for a website development project will depend on a variety of factors unique to each project.

Phase 4: Further Improvement 
 
This all depends on the client if they want an ongoing update or a one-time deal. A website should never be considered finished; we charge clients $1,500 per month for continuously improving the conversion rate of their website and building a landing page each month.

If you want a membership platform in your website or a learning platform we also build that using MemberPress: a WordPress membership site plugin and LMS that makes it easy to charge your users for access to content and digital products such as software, e-books, and online courses you create.

Website Maintenance

We offer ongoing maintenance and support service for your website starting at $500.

Website Audit

The main objective of a website audit is to evaluate the website's overall performance and identify any technical or strategic issues that may be hindering its effectiveness. A website audit may cost different amounts of money based on things like how complicated the website is, how much the audit covers, and how much information is needed. 

Starting at $1,000 monthly.

Usability Testing

Through website usability testing, businesses can evaluate the ways their customers are interacting with their website and spot any potential problems that may interfere with the user's overall experience. 

Starting at $1,000 monthly.

GROWTH DRIVEN WEBSITE REDESIGN

Growth Driven Design (GDD) is a process of continuously improving your website as you collect data from your website users. That is, as your site visitors interact with the website, you then take that information you get from their experience to create an improved version of your website.

So, rather than redesigning a new version of your website every two years you can instead make gradual changes based on how people interact with it.

This will save you time and money from having to redesign your entire website every year - wasting valuable time and resources because you never know if it's going to convert site visitors or help them with what they need.

Price: $12,000 6 months contract

Price: $20,000 1 year contract

CONVERSION RATE OPTIMIZATION

We increase the percentage of conversion from our client’s website by generating new ideas and identifying areas for improvement based on users' interaction with the website. This knowledge is then used to generate theories through A/B and multivariate testing.

Starting at $2,000 monthly.
